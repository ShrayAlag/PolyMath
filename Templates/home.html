Polymath is a platform that enables you to get smart summaries and learn about interconnected pieces of information. You can also use the platform to get a smart summary for any piece of information whenever you need it. The name "polymath" means one who is fluent in several fields of knowledge. Examples of famous polymaths include Ben Franklin and Nicolaus Copernicus. Happy learning 😊.
